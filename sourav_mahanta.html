The Artist

Born in Guwahati, Assam, Sourav Jyoti Mahanta has been rendering vocals and music for cinema, television, and the stage since 1998 — weaving together the breadth of India's musical heritage with the emotional demands of modern filmmaking.

He holds a five-year Visharad degree in Indian Classical Vocal, Tabla, and Assamese Traditional Khol Instrument. His training spans the classical lineages of Guru Ananda Mohan Bhagawati and Guru Kesavananda Deva Goswami — luminaries of Assamese Sattriya and folk traditions — alongside Western Classical theory at the Shillong School of Music under Trinity Guide Hall & Rock School of Music, London.

He received his Ph.D. from the Department of Communication & Journalism, Gauhati University, and continues to serve as Guest Music Faculty at Dr. Bhupen Hazarika Regional Government Film & Television Institute (Jyoti Chitraban), Guwahati.

Domains of Work

🎬
Film Music Direction

Complete music direction and composition for feature films — from original scores to song music — spanning Assamese, Karbi, and Hindi cinema. Multiple state and national award–winning collaborations.

🎵
Background Score

Atmospheric and emotionally resonant background scores for cinema and television, including works shown at MAMI, IFFLA Los Angeles, and the Dhaka International Film Festival.

🎤
Playback Vocals

A seasoned playback singer with nominations at the Prag Cine Awards and Assam State Film Awards, and regular Assamese modern song broadcasts on All India Radio since 2010.

🏛️
Classical & Folk Music

Deep mastery of Sattriya, Assamese folk, Hindustani classical, and Borgeet traditions. Performed at Sangeet Natak Academy events, SAARC Cultural Meets, and international theater festivals.

📺
Television & OTT

Music composition for 13 television serials, 1 OTT web series, more than 15 radio plays and dramas, bringing cinematic texture and emotional depth to the small screen.

🎹
Music Education

Faculty at Dr. Bhupen Hazarika Regional Govt. Film & Television Institute, shaping the next generation of film musicians with a curriculum rooted in both classical and contemporary practice.
